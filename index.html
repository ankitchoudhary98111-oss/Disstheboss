PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

Photos

Photos you upload are processed entirely on your device. They are NEVER uploaded to our servers, never stored, never shared.

AI Caricatures

When generating an AI caricature, a text description is sent to OpenAI's API. No photo is sent. No identifiable personal data is retained. The result is a fictional cartoon, not a realistic representation.

AI Writing

AI vent and roast writing uses Anthropic Claude API. Text prompts generate fictional content only.

Data We Collect

Email (if you sign up voluntarily). Anonymous vents/roasts (no personally identifiable info). Google Analytics (anonymous usage stats).

Your Rights

Email disstheboss@gmail.com to delete your data at any time.
