ABOUT US

DissTheBoss was born out of one simple truth: work can be frustrating, bosses can be maddening, and sometimes you just need to throw something — even if it's virtual.

Our Mission

We believe every worker deserves a safe, anonymous, and fun outlet for workplace stress. DissTheBoss is that outlet. No real people are harmed. No real names are used. Just pure, satisfying stress relief through games and community.

What We Offer

🎯 Boss Dart Board — Throw darts at your virtual boss and score points based on accuracy.

🎱 Boss Pool — Pocket all 9 toxic boss types in this satisfying billiards game.

💬 Anonymous Vent Feed — Share your workplace frustrations anonymously with thousands of others who totally get it.

🎡 More Games Coming — Spin the Wheel, Whack-A-Boss and more launching soon.

Who We Are

DissTheBoss is an entertainment platform built for the millions of workers around the world who deserve better. We are not affiliated with any employer or HR department. Everything here is fictional satire for entertainment purposes only.
