ABOUT US

DissTheBoss was born from one truth: work can be frustrating, bosses can be maddening, and sometimes you need to throw something — even if it's virtual.

Our Mission

A safe, anonymous, funny outlet for workplace stress. Everything is fictional satire for entertainment only.

AI Features

DissTheBoss uses AI writing powered by Claude (Anthropic) and OpenAI to generate funny fictional vent content. All AI content is for entertainment only. AI caricatures are cartoon illustrations — not realistic representations of real people.

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

Photos & Images

Photos you upload are processed entirely on your device in your browser. They are NEVER uploaded to our servers, never stored, never shared. They exist only in your browser session memory.

AI Caricatures

When generating an AI caricature (paid feature), your text description is sent to OpenAI's API. No identifiable personal data is retained. OpenAI's privacy policy applies. The result is a fictional cartoon, not a realistic representation.

AI Writing

AI vent and roast writing uses Anthropic Claude API. Text prompts generate fictional content. No personal data is stored.

Data We Collect

Email address (if you sign up voluntarily). Anonymous vents/roasts (no personally identifiable info).

Analytics

Google Analytics for anonymous usage stats. Opt out via browser settings.

Your Rights

Email disstheboss@gmail.com to delete your data.
